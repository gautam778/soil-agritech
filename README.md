The Agricultural productivity greatly
depends on the quality of soils, the presence of nutrients and the
way in which you avoid or handle the process of irrigation. As
food production increases and water is a scarce resource, we
need precision agriculture as the key to sustainable farming. The
traditional modes of farming are based on material observation
and fixed patterns of irrigation that tend to result into wastage
of water, inefficient utilization of resources and decline in
production. The methods do not fit the changes occurring in the
soil and natural conditions in a real-time and this hinders
effectiveness. To ease these problems, a new novel suggests a Soil
AgriTech system comprised of integrating IoT-based soil
sensation and machine learning in monitoring real-time and
suggesting irrigation in intelligent manner. It obtains the
parameters like the soil moisture, temperature, PH, and
nutrient documentation and creates data-based irrigation
choices utilizing the model of the Random Forests. Chatbot
interface that the farmers could chat with the system and receive
soil-health information and irrigation advice in a user-friendly
manner. As compared to more conservative irrigation methods,
the suggested system makes possible dynamic and data-driven
decision-making like the current state of the modern precision
agriculture solutions and enhances the conservation of water
and assists more efficient crop control in the real working
conditions of farms.
